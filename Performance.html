Performance

Definition

A performance is an Avant-garde show in which elements from various arts and fields are combined, such as music, dance, theater and plastic arts. has its origins in Futurism and Dadaism but it became a major phenomenon in the 60s and 70s and can be seen as a branch of Conceptual Art.

It is characterized by the combnation between extravangance, questioning, abstract and for leaving more to the viewer to draw their own conclusions.
